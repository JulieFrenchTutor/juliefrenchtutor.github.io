About Me

I’ve been immersed in the French language and culture since I was a child. I attended the French Lycée from the age of 4 all the way through to A levels, and then went on to study French at university. I also spent a year living in Paris, which deepened my love for the language and gave me first-hand experience of French life.

My journey as a tutor began ten years ago when I started helping my daughter with her French GCSE. This quickly grew into supporting her school friends as well, and before long, word of mouth helped me build a thriving tutoring business.

Today, I tutor students from age 11 through to GCSE, A-Level, and university level. I’m passionate about helping each student grow in confidence and skill, tailoring lessons to suit their individual needs and goals.

Services

  • One-on-one French tutoring (online or in-person)
  • GCSE and A-Level French exam preparation
  • Conversational practice for adults
  • Customized lessons for beginners to advanced levels

Why Learn with Me?

Whether you're preparing for school exams, brushing up for travel, or learning French for professional reasons, I offer highly personalised lessons that make real progress possible — no matter your starting point.

  • Native fluency: I’m a native French speaker and completely bilingual in French and English, offering authentic language use and cultural depth in every session.
  • Dedicated French specialist: I tutor only French — no other subjects — allowing me to focus exclusively on delivering the highest-quality French instruction.
  • Tailored to ability: I adapt lessons to each learner — identifying and filling knowledge gaps in less confident students, while pushing advanced learners beyond the exam syllabus.
  • Support for adults too: I also work with adult learners — whether you're starting from scratch, learning for pleasure, or need to build your confidence in French for work or relocation.
  • Friendly and focused: I combine a warm, encouraging teaching style with clear expectations and structure — helping students stay motivated and reach their full potential.
